Emergency Numbers Useful Emergency Numbers

911

Dial if you're facing a life-threatening emergency. More information List of emergencies for 911 include:
  • Crimes in progress (not if already occurred)
  • Life-threatening situation
  • Fires or smoke in the building
  • Explosive Devices
  • Traffic accidents with injuries
  • Aircraft emergencies
  • Cliff rescues
  • Fuel or Hazardous chemical spills
  • Beach or water-related emergency

Poison Control

1-800-222-1222

Contact Poison Control if you swallow or come in contact with a substance that might be poisonous. In case of a collapse, seizure, or trouble breathing, call 911 right away.

Animal Poison Control

1-888-426-4435

Contact the Animal Poison Control Center for any animal poison-related emergency, or if you think your pet may have ingested a potentially poisonous substance.
