Coastal Flooding Methodology The Coastal Flooding Hazard Index is based on three key components: the mean elevation of your block, the expected sea level rise in your area by 2050, and your hurricane risk.Note that Storm surges are currently not described. Depending on your location, storm surges can increase the water level by considerable amounts.

Augurisk Now shows a threat whenever the score is higher than 70.

Sources: Elevation data - USGS 3DEP Elevation program.
Sweet, W.V., R.E. Kopp, C. P. Weaver, J. Obeysekera, R. M. Horton, E.R. Thieler and C. Zervas (2017), Global and Regional Sea Level Rise Scenarios for the United States. NOAA Tech. Rep. NOS CO-OPS 83.
