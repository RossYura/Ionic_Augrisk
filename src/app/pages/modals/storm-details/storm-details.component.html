Storm Events Methodology A probabilistic approach is applied using data from the NOAA natural hazard database. The probabilistic component is associated to an impact measure to define a county-level Storm event hazard index, based on several hazard components: Tornadoes, Hurricanes, Blizzards, Snow Storms, and Hail Storms.

Augurisk Now shows a threat whenever the score is higher than 70.

Source: NOAA National Centers for Environmental Information: Storm Events database.
