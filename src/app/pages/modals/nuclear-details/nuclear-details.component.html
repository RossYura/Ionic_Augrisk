Nuclear Radiation Methodology The Augurisk Nuclear Hazard index is defined at the U.S. Census block level. It takes into account the distance and characteristics of nearby nuclear power plants, including reactor capacity, age and existence of a nearby nuclear waste storage site. All values are normalized to a 0-100 scale.

Augurisk Now shows a threat whenever the score is higher than 70.

Sources: U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission’s high-value datasets, Lelieveld, J., D. Kunkel, and M.G. Lawrence. 2012., Atmospheric Chemistry and Physics 12(9):4245–4258.
